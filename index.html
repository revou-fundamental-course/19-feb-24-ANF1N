ABOUT OUR HOSPITAL

About Us

WakandaHospital is a leading healthcare institution towering majestically in the heart of Surabaya city. As one of the top hospitals in healthcare delivery, WakandaHospital has set high standards in holistic and innovative medical care.

As a symbol of innovation, care, and high-quality service, WakandaHospital continues to be a cornerstone in the community in providing the best healthcare. With its rich history and unwavering dedication to the community's well-being, the hospital remains a beacon of hope for all seeking comprehensive and individual-focused care.

History

Established in 1975 by Dr.Richard, a visionary physician committed to revolutionizing healthcare, WakandaHospital began as a humble clinic providing basic medical services to the local community. However, Dr. T'Challa's vision extended far beyond. With unwavering determination and community support, the clinic blossomed into a leading hospital within a short span.


Over the years, WakandaHospital has evolved and undergone remarkable transformations. Emphasizing innovation, research, and patient-centric care, the hospital has become a center of excellence in the medical field. Visionary leadership and a dedicated medical team have helped build WakandaHospital's reputation as a trusted healthcare institution.

Vision And Mission

"To become the leading healthcare center that provides the best service with state-of-the-art technology and patient-focused care to enhance the quality of life for the community."


Mission

1. To provide high-quality, holistic, and affordable healthcare services to every patient.

2. To adopt the latest technology and best practices in medicine for diagnosis, treatment, and care.

3. To prioritize patient safety, comfort, and satisfaction in every interaction with medical and non-medical staff.

4. To develop and support disease prevention programs and public health education to increase awareness of the importance of healthy lifestyles.

5. To engage in medical research and collaborate with scientific institutions to continually improve service standards and discover more effective therapies.
